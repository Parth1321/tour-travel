Gwalior fort

Located in Madhya Pradesh, India, is an imposing hilltop fortress with a rich history dating back over a thousand years.

It features impressive architecture, including palaces, temples, and intricate carvings, reflecting various dynasties that ruled the region.

Khajuraho Temples

Located in Madhya Pradesh, India, are renowned for their exquisite carvings and intricate sculptures, representing both divine and earthly life

Built between the 9th and 11th centuries by the Chandela dynasty, these temples are a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Sanchi Stupa

Located in Madhya Pradesh, India, is one of the oldest stone structures in India, built by Emperor Ashoka in the 3rd century BCE.

It is a significant Buddhist monument, featuring a large hemispherical dome that enshrines relics of the Buddha

Pachmarhi

Often called the "Queen of Satpura," is a hill station in Madhya Pradesh, known for its scenic beauty and spiritual significance.

It is home to the Jata Shankar and Mahadeo Temples, which are dedicated to Lord Shiva and are nestled amidst caves and lush forests.

Amarkantak

Amarkantak is known as the king of pilgrimages, or Tirthraj, because the town has many temples and holy places.

A revered pilgrimage destination, known as the origin of the holy rivers Narmada and Son. The Amarkantak Temples, including the Narmada Kund and ancient Kalachuri-era shrines, attract devotees seeking spiritual solace.

panna tiger reserve

Panna National Park is an Indian national park in Panna and Chhatarpur Districts of Madhya Pradesh with an area of 542.67 km.It was declared as one of the Tiger reserves of India in 1994/95 and placed under the protection of Project Tiger.

Park features diverse landscapes, including teak forests, grasslands, and the Ken River

bhedaghat

Bhedaghat, located near Jabalpur in Madhya Pradesh, is famous for its stunning marble rocks that tower over the Narmada River.

The site is renowned for the Dhuandhar Falls, where the river cascades down in a smoky mist.Bhedaghat is a popular destination for boat rides.
